About Me

Hey, I'm Andrew! I'm a (recent) graduate with a bachelor's degree in economics, and am a current engineering student in the University of Waterloo's Systems Design Engineering program. My interests mainly lie in the space between economics and engineering — stuff like industrial engineering and infrastructure systems — and my work experience is mainly various flavours of data analysis. That said, I'm also interested in embedded systems and low-level computer systems, electronics, game design, woodworking, machinery, and more.

This site is a place for me to work on ideas, share things I've learned, and show off things I've done. If that sounds interesting, read ahead and check back! But don't check back too soon, I'm still trying to figure out how write frequently.

If you'd like some more details of my experience and/or qualifications, you can read my CV here!
